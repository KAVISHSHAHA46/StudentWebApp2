School Achievments

And we see results in students’ skills in math, reading, composition, public speaking, self-management, and independent thinking.
At Sanskriti we are committed to providing a safe and secure learning environment to enable our children to develop skills that will help them be caring, confident and responsible global citizens. As Principal of Sanskriti I believe in the power of the young mind to make a change. It is with this belief and faith that we endeavor to inculcate in our children values of empathy, responsibility, humility and the passion to believe in their dreams.
Sanskriti nurtures talents in the performing arts, visual arts, sports and academics. We provide a platform for these young minds to discover themselves and make choices for the future. The impressive performance of the students in the board examinations is an evidence of the school’s commitment to maintaining the highest standards in teaching and learning. As an inclusive school our dedicated team of teachers support children through their challenges and help each one reach their potential. We believe in “No child being left behind” and work with determination to achieve this aim.
At Sanskriti children learn to be tolerant, supportive and accepting of differences in abilities and beliefs. These values will take them beyond the narrow definitions of success and achievement. As the world moves towards unsafe and unsettled times we will continue to provide to our students lessons in the kindness, courage and generosity of the human race and its ability to reconstruct and rebuild. Frequent and focused workshops both for teachers and students ensure that we stay abreast of the latest research in field of education. In addition the school has a tradition of student and teacher exchanges to build awareness of varied perspectives.
We hope that our children will be the artists, musicians and diplomats who will strive to bring peace to this world, the environmentalists who will work to reverse climate change and doctors who will eradicate disease from the face of this earth. We are also fortunate to have “The Civil Services Society “as a parent organization and engaged and involved parents who encourage and support the children through this journey. We are a school community that firmly believes in this partnership between students, teachers and parents and works with consensus, transparency and accountability. We look forward to their continued support as Sanskriti continues to develop and grow as a centre of excellence.
